Day #3: Doubly linked list

Day-to-day the challenges get bigger, the data structures get more complicated, but I will try my best to publish the new articles before 9PM (GMT+1).
Today I implemented the doubly linked list, with a small twist, there's no random-access, rather I made it like a two-ended stack, you can push and pop at the two ends. With some clever algorithms, this DS could make for a great median-finding with many additions/removals. (We have O(1) addition and removal on both ends, and we could get a median with some small changes wiht O(n/2).
